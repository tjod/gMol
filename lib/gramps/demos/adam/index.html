ADAM

This is a demo of A Dial Activated Man (ADAM) Instead of dials, we use a mouse now, but AMAM doesn't cut it. You can rotate the entire scene using the left mouse button.

The mouse wheel and right button can perform various actions. Some of these actions are poses or animations, after which the mouse wheel and right button are not active.
