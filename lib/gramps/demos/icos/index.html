Construction of an icosohedron

This creates an icosohedon from an equiliateral triangle whose center is at the origin (x,y,z) = (0,0,0) and whose sides have length=1. Based on this location and dimension, certain rotations and translations are applied to 20 instances of this triangle creating an icosohedron, with each of the 20 faces being one of the copies.
